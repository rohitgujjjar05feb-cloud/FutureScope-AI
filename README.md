# FutureScope AI – Startup Mentor RAG

## Overview

FutureScope AI is a startup mentoring platform that helps aspiring entrepreneurs evaluate and improve their startup ideas.

The system provides:

* Market Analysis
* Competitor Analysis
* Funding Opportunities
* Risk Assessment
* Startup Roadmap

## Problem Statement

Many aspiring entrepreneurs struggle to validate startup ideas, understand market opportunities, identify competitors, and find funding sources.

FutureScope AI helps users analyze startup concepts and generate actionable business insights.

## Features

### Market Analysis

Provides insights into potential market opportunities and growth areas.

### Competitor Analysis

Helps identify existing competitors and market gaps.

### Funding Opportunities

Suggests possible funding sources such as angel investors, venture capital firms, incubators, and government programs.

### Risk Assessment

Highlights business, operational, and market-related risks.

### Startup Roadmap

Provides a structured path from idea validation to product launch.
